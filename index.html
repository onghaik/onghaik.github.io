    Hi!

    I am a second-year PhD student at Princeton University within the Department of Operations Research and Financial Engineering. Currently, my research focuses on modeling the impact of data center growth on electricity prices and using techniques from stochastic control to inform grid planning. I am also interested in risk-sharing mechanisms for renewables to hedge against intermittency.

    Previously, I received my B.S. in Mathematics (with a Specialization in Computing) from UCLA.
